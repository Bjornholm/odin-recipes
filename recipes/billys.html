Billys pan pizza

A great premade pizza snack, ready to heat!

This rectangular pizza is a staple amongst youth in Sweden. It's a smaller portion and can be heated in the oven or microwave.

ingredients

  • However many Billys pan pizzas you are craving
  • Thats it, no cutlery, plates or condiments required

instructions

  1. Rip corner of of the Billys package
  2. Microwave it for about 2 minutes on high setting, less if your really hungry and want to eat a cold pizza
  3. That's it, it's ready to eat right out of the package
  4. Repeat steps 1 to 3 as many times as necessary
