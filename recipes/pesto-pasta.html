Pesto pasta

Delicious pasta with chicken and pesto

Al dente penne and chunks of tender, juicy chicken breast are slicked with a creamy pesto sauce thatâ€™s bursting with herby, garlicky flavor. The brightness of the pesto plus a full bag of baby spinach keeps the richness of the creamy sauce in check.

ingredients

  • cloves of garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • chicken breasts
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • dried penne or other tube shaped pasta
  • olive oil
  • baby spinach
  • heavy cream
  • pesto
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)

instructions

  1. salt water and bring it to boil
  2. mince garlic, grate parmesan
  3. add pasta to boiling water read the package for serving size and time!
  4. heat frying pan with olive oil in, add chicken breasts
  5. season chicken with salt and pepper as desired
  6. When pasta is done and chicken is ready add some pasta water to frying pan
  7. mix everything else in frying pan
  8. done, eat
